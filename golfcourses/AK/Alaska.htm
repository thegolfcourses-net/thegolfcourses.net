Golf Courses in Alaska

 

Anchorage Golf Course

Although there are  only about 25 golf courses throughout the great state of Alaska because of the extremely short golfing season, the ones you’ll find there can be magnificent!  In particular, we recommend Anchorage Golf Course where you can even book your tee time online right from this website and save money!

You will also find many fine golf courses in picturesque settings with gigantic mountain scenery nearby in or near other cities in Alaska, including Fairbanks, Haines, Juneau, Sitka, and Soldotna
