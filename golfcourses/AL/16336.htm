The Legislator Course at Robert Trent Jones Golf Trail At Capitol Hill Golf Course is an 18-hole regulation length golf course in Prattville, Alabama. This championship layout features 4 sets of teeboxes for the enjoyment of golfers of all skill levels. There are 2 other courses on this same property, the Senator Course, and The Judge Course, and the Sherling Course.
