Golf Courses in Alabama

Soldiers Creek Golf Club At Woerner Preserve

 

 

Alabama is a beautiful golf course state, largely because of the Robert Trent Jones Golf Trail courses, which consists of 11 different locations throughout the state of Alabama, each location with multiple courses and each of them designed by the most famous golf course architect of all time, Robert Trent Jones.  Of particular interest is the Fighting Joe Course at The Shoals in Muscle Shoals because of the fact that this particular course was the first to break the 8,000 yard barrier in yardage from the back tees.

There are many other beautiful places to play golf throughout Alabama, so browse our list and make a tee time today!
