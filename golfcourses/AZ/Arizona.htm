Golf Courses in Arizona

Scottsdale Silverado Golf Club

You can’t beat being in the sun and sand of an Arizona golf course!!! Many require using a golf cart and as a result seem to go fast. And many, like the courses in Scottsdale, showcase the beauty of the desert sky! All feature the qualities that can only be found in Arizona. Many have the famous cacti and mountain peaks with an abundance of sunshine. Imagine experiencing the thrill of playing golf in the pictures you’ve seen in Arizona Highways —What a combination!!!! Make your tee time early for the most popular courses as they fill up fast. And many have twilight rates that provide substantial savings and the chance to relax without the pressure of a crowded course.
