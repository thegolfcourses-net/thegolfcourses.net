Golf Courses in California

Hiddenbrooke Golf Club in Vallejo, CA

California offers many spectacular opportunities for golfers. It has perhaps the most perfect weather year-around. It gives a golfer the opportunity to play some of the nation’s premier and most famous courses. You can play Pebble Beach or Torrey Pines, or the Murietta courses. Each offers a challenge of a lifetime, especially if the famous seacoast winds pick up. Be prepared and check out conditions so you can change your swing to compensate for the wind. Enjoy breathtaking views on the coast. Every skill level is available across the state… and don’t forget to check for what is available on our on-line tee-time service…We can save you money on your trip.
