Pebble Beach Area Golf Courses
Includes Monterey, Seaside, Carmel, Watsonville, and Aptos

The Monterey Peninsula has some of the most beautiful ocean-front views on a 17-mile drive, and contains some of the most prestigious golf courses in the country. It is best known as the sight of the famous PGA Tour’s Pebble Beach Golf Tournament. The area is saturated with magnificent locations for golf courses.

In this area is The Laguna Seca Golf Ranch. It is a course designed by Robert Trent Jones Sr. and Jr., and is situated down in the Monterey-Salinas Corridor. Already an 18-hole championship course, it also provides a top rate golf practice area.

The Carmel area has the Rancho Canada Golf Club. Its two courses meander back and forth across the Carmel River and have some of the finest holes in the area.
