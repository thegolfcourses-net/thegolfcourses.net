Golf Courses in Florida

17th hole at TPC Sawgrass

Talk about golf!!!! Florida offers everything a golfer could want!! Every type of terrain or level of golf is available. Take the family to the Orlando attractions and enjoy that area’s courses. Throughout the state you find a golf heaven!!! There you can learn the sport by playing the sport. Don’t forget!! Golf is the GAME in Florida. Try the Legends Golf and Country Club, The Orange Lake Resort courses or the Palm Beach National Golf & Country Club, depending on where you are! There is plenty of courses to pick from! The thing that surprises most people is that golf on many courses is cheap, except perhaps in busy high seasons around Jan.-Feb! It is basically a golfer’s market. There are so many courses everywhere that each course does all it can to give you a good deal for your money!
