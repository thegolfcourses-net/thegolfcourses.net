Golf Courses in Iowa

Bos Landen Golf Resort, Pella, IA

Iowa golf—- you can just smell the fresh air. There are few distractions found in many of the states courses. And the comfortable hometown people can’t be beat!!! You can find it a real joy to golf in Iowa!!! There is a huge quantity to pick from.

Try the bluffs of Decorah for spectacular beauty at the historic old country club, or if you want to be more metropolitan go to Des Moines. There you will find the Waveland Course, a very fine public facility.

Cedar Rapids has six public courses that provide a wonderful experience. Very popular in Iowa are two-person best ball tournaments. The top 30% win prizes, so remember to take friend and practice up!!
