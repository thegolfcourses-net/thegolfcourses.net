The Lakewood Course at St. Andrews Golf & Country Club, Inc. is an 18-hole regulation length golf course in West Chicago, Illinois. This medium-length layout has 3 sets of teeboxes for a fun, but challenging golfing experience. There is another course on this same property, the St. Andrews Course. Online tee times may be available at St. Andrews Golf & Country Club, Inc. or at nearby golf courses, usually offered at a discount from the normal rate.
