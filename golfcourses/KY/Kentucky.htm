Golf Courses in Kentucky

Fox Run Golf Course, Independence, KY

Louisville Golf in Kentucky is famous for persimmon clubs made since 1974. They have become world famous. Although most people have switched to metal clubs, the use of them is like stepping back in time. It creates a lower launch with decent distance and greater sense of feel. Connoisseurs of clubs find them very desirable. So give them a try as you play one of Kentucky’s fine courses like Old Silo Golf Club, in the Lexington area, or Chariot Run Golf Club, in the Louisville area. If it matters, remember as you head to the course to check whether it’s in a wet or dry county, because they vary from county to county.
