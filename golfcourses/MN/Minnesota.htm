Golf Courses in Minnesota

Mississippi Dunes Golf Links, Cottage Grove, MN

Minnesota has a great deal to offer a golfer. There is a wide variety of terrain across the state. In the Spring, be sure to bring the jacket as you could be caught in one the many cloudbursts that hit that time of year. They are magnificent if you happen to come from one of the drought states, and are part of the reason for the rich greenery found at Minnesota clubs. One of the premier courses is the Legacy Complex in Brainerd. Located in the center of the state, these courses are Robert Trent Jones designs, and have been labeled as some of the “Best Environmental Courses in the World”. Its beauty is hard to match. There are few cities that offer as many courses as the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul. We might suggest Eagle Valley Golf Course for its reasonable rates.
