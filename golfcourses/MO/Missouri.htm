Golf Courses in Missouri

Indian Rock Golf Club, Laurie, MO

One of the best areas in Missouri to play golf in is the Lake Ozark Area. Take advantage of our low tee times rates there as well as the St. Louis, Kansas City, and Springfield areas. Once considered a state with a peaceful undisturbed population, Missouri has recently gained recognition for its moderate weather. It has grown in recent years and become a vacation destination at all times of the year. The result is that golf has grown as an industry with many fine courses. In Branson you will find the Branson Creek Course, although more pricey at about $100, it is considered one of Missouri’s best courses. If you’re in the St. Louis area try playing Quail Creek Golf Course. It was designed by Hale Erwin, and is a 72-par course that challenges all golf levels. Remember that you are in the Ozark Mountains area and weather can be volatile, so carry your umbrella and heed all weather warnings.
