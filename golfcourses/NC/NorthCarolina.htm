Golf Courses in North Carolina

Falling Creek Golf Club, Kinston, NC

Many of the courses in North Carolina will challenge the experienced golfer. Since many are over 50 years old, they have developed character and trees that present problems for a powerful golfer. They help develop a disciplined swing and the willpower to navigate the narrow fairways, but the experience is well worth it. The most famous course is Pinehurst. Although the cost is expensive, the experience that has plagued and gratified many famous players can be yours! Also, check out Pine Knolls near Greensboro or Crooked Creek near Raleigh/Durham for a good game of golf!

Also we can highly recommend the course pictured at left, Falling Creek Golf Club, for a high end golf outing at a reasonable price if you book through us!
