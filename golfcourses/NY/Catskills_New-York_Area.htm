Catskills!

The Catskills area offers many a fine opportunity for golf for New York residents and visitors alike.

At the Concord Resort and Golf Club, in Kiamesha Lake, NY, you'll find an opportunity to play The Monster Course, a golf experience designed to challenge the heavy hitters of the game! At 7650 yards from the back championship tees, it would help if you were a big bruiser of a man capable of developing very fast clubhead speed, in order to reach the greens in regulation. A course rating of 76.8 reflects the length of this course, but also, with a slope rating of 137, you can be sure that there are difficulties along the way in the form of water and bunkers.
If banging out 300+ yard drives isn't within your capabilities or your desires, you might have more fun at Tarry Brae Municipal, in South Fallsburg, NY, a layout of only 6,888 yards from the back tees, but still, with a formidable course rating of 73.4 and slope of 129. The fairways at Tarry Brae are tree-lined and there are lots of bunkers guarding the greens and some water hazards as well. Some of the 3-par holes on this 18 hole course are lengthy, over 200 yards and uphill too!
The Lochmor Golf Course in Loch Sheldrake, NY offers an experience that gives you pine trees lining some of the fairways as well as many oak trees. The greens are fast, small, and well taken care of. 18 holes at a par of 71 and a length of 6,550 yards promises a fun round of golf for everyone.
Tennanah Lake Golf and Tennis Club in Roscoe, NY is the oldest course around and also sits in a location with the highest elevation in Sullivan County. Another toughy, this one stretches about 6800 yards from the tips, has a course rating of 73.7 and a slope rating of 132.
