Walnut Run Golf Course, Cortland, OH

Now Available – Toledo Tee Times

Golf in Ohio has grown in recent years as courses have worked to challenge the players, while many rates have stayed low to encourage their play. Grey Hawk Golf Course in Lagrange, OH, offers “a championship course built to USGA specifications” with ten lakes and a meandering stream certain to challenge your skills. The Golf Club at Stonelick Hills in Batavia is another course worth checking out! Greens on the Ohio courses may be either bent grass or bermuda so check on which it is at a particular course. Remember, putting might be a little different depending on grass type.
