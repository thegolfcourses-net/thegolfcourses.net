Cimarron Trails, Perkins, OK

Ever have a group of five golfers and one of your buddies gets left out? Well, not so at the Jimmie Austin OU Golf Course in Norman. Monday thru Thursday they allow fivesomes with rates peaking at $40 each. Twilight rates bring it down to about $100 for the group!!! Can’t beat it!!! If you want to golf at an immaculate course, try Forest Ridge at Broken Arrow near Tulsa. Remember, in Oklahoma it gets hot in the summer so plan ahead. Think about taking advantage of twilight rates. Carry plenty of water and an umbrella, and use sunscreen for UVA and UVB rays.

Please don’t miss playing at The Golf Club at Cimarron Trails, pictured at left, which is located near Stillwater. A very scenic course, Cimarron Trails was designed by Kevin Benedict and features Bent Grass greens and Bermuda Grass fairways.
