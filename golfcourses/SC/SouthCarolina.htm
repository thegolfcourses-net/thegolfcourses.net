Island Green Golf Club, Myrtle Beach, SC

Myrtle Beach Central, South, and North Areas!

Myrtle Beach and golf are synonymous when it comes to golf in South Carolina. There are activities for the whole family so it is a perfect destination to play golf and spend family time. With the beautiful beaches, what could be a more romantic honeymoon!!! Check out Wild Wing Plantation where all levels of play are available at this 45-hole tract.. You can travel down the coast to visit famous Hilton Head Island. It is another golf destination and is well worth the drive. The state has a humid subtropical climate, with 85 golf courses. One populous area in S.C. is Charleston, (350,209) A golf course near there is Legend Oaks Plantation Golf Club, which was recently named South Carolina Golf Course of the Year. Legend Oaks also has a renowned tennis facility. Another main golf course is the awesome area Myrtle Beach with more than 100 golf courses with some of the best layouts for all skill levels. Tee off in North, South, or Central Myrtle Beach! Interesting to know South Carolina has a AAA minor baseball league called “The Charlotte Knights!”
