Golf Courses in South Dakota

The Bluffs Golf Course, Vermillion, SD

South Dakota has a number of very nice courses including the one pictured here to the left, The Bluffs, in Vermillion, SD. If you only have time for 9 holes, we suggest that you try and make it over to Lakes Golf Course in Wentworth, which is located about half way between the I-29 North of Sioux Falls and the city of Madison to the West and near Lake Madison.

Edgebrook Golf Course in Brookings is another fine choice and sports 18 holes with a par of 70 and reasonable green fees of $24 and sometimes even as low as $16.80 as of August 2014. Moving a little farther West and North, you’ll discover Broadland Creek National Golf Course, near Huron, where the gorgeous countryside setting will assure you of a wonderful 18 holes of golf.

The Rapid City area, on the Western side of the state has many nice courses for you to explore as well.
