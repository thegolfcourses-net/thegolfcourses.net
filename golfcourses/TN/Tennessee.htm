Golf Courses in Tennessee

Southern Hills Golf & Country Club, Cookeville, TN

Tennessee is best known for golf as the home of the “Bear Trace” set of courses designed by Jack Nicklaus. There are five courses that bear his name and style. Bear Trace – Cumberland Mountain in Crossville is one of them. Many offer many greenside bunkers and. plenty of sand to challenge your shots. Other courses to experience are Quail Ridge Golf Club, River Islands Golf Course, and Gatlinburg Golf Course in Pigeon Forge. One of the greatest features of golf in Tennessee is the rates fall below that of other states so you can golf often for your money…
