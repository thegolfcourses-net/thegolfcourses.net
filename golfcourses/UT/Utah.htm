Golf Courses in Utah

The Hideout Golf Club, Monticello, UT

In Utah, scenery will steal you away from your game if you don’t watch out. With the blazing red rocks and the deep canyons of Southern Utah, you’ll have much to see. Many of the courses capture this personality with slopes found on many of the greens. Start your tour with Moab Golf Club in Moab. Don’t miss the growing golf area of St. George, and while you’re in that area, slip over the state line to Nevada and enjoy the Mesquite area, only 30 miles from St. George. If you want a more metropolis feel, go to Salt Lake City. For a change of pace try going to Utah’s oldest golf course at Forest Dale Golf Club. It is a 100 year old 9-hole course and can be played for a mere $20. Talk about stepping back in history!!!! Enjoy your game, and take heed to notice many of the courses have challenging green locations.
