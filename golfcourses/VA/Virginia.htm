Golf Courses in Virginia

Sycamore Creek Golf Course, Manakin-Sabot, VA

Virginia is a wonderful place to enjoy the beauty of nature while golfing. Many discounts are available throughout the state. The wonder of golfing in Virginia in the spring cannot be matched!! When the azaleas and the rhododendrons begin to bloom the look is exquisite!!! Play at Sycamore Creek (pictured at left), in Manakin-Sabot, just a little Northwest of Richmond for an entralling golf experience. Try the Birdwood Course at the University of Virginia, Charlottesville, for a sampling of this beauty…Enjoy its loveliness…. and the challenge of its play…

Mariners Landing Country Club in Huddleston, VA is another fine choice because it was designed by Robert Trent Jones, Sr. and because of its lakeside setting with sailboats sailing on that lake.
