Golf Courses in Washington

Eaglemont Golf Club, Mount Vernon, WA

Washington has an abundance of good golf courses and beautiful settings. The state has 3 distinct popular golf areas. The Seattle/Coastal area, Central Washington, and the Spokane area, which spills over into Idaho to include Couer D Alene. The golfer will find the weather is playable nearly year around. It is best to take the time to warm up during the cold season. Every location in the state is within a short distance of multiple courses. Some of the best known clubs in the Seattle area are Willows Run and Trilogy Golf Club at Redmond Ridge. In the Spokane area there is the Chewelah Golf & Country Club, which can be booked online sometimes for a great savings over the usual rate. Two great courses in Central Washington are Canyon Lakes in the Tri-Cities area, and Leavenworth Golf Club, in Leavenworth, WA.
