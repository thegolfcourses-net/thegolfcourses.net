Golf Courses in Wisconsin

Ridges Golf Course, Wisconsin Rapids, WI

Believe it or not, in Wisconsin you can enjoy the pleasure of golf in one form or another all year around! Thanks to Madison’s Vitense’s Golfland. It’s a 20,000 square foot facility called a golfer’s amusement park offering a driving range, lessons and a 3-par course. It also offers a climbing wall and batting cages. It’s perfect for that athletic person in your life and open year around. Wisconsin offers many low cost golf rates at good courses. Check out the courses in the Geneva Lakes Area, such as The Brute at Grand Geneva, the beautiful Madison area, and the Green Bay Area. These areas are some of the most gorgeous golfing destinations in the country, not to mention the state of Wisconsin. Once again this state has many places of natural woodland beauty. However, if you’re in the Milwaukee Area, you won’t want to miss The Golf Club at Camelot or Quit-Qui-Oc in Elkhart Lake.
